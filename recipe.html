Roasted Chicken Wings

A delicious food, it smell good, looks very cryspy, and it was just take you about 90 minutes.

Things needed

  • 3 to 4 lbs party wings (flats and drumettes).
  • 1.5 Tbsp aluminum-free baking powder.
  • 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp onion powder,1 tsp smoked paprika, and ½ tsp black pepper.
  • ½ cup melted butter + ½ cup Frank’s RedHot Sauce (or your favorite BBQ sauce).

Perpare

  • Place the wings in a large bowl and use paper towels to pat them completely dry.
  • In a small bowl, mix the baking powder and all your dry spices. Sprinkle this mixture evenly over the wings and toss until every piece is coated.

Cook

  • Place a wire baking rack inside a rimmed baking sheet (line the bottom sheet with foil for easy cleanup). Place the wings in a single layer, making sure they aren’t touching, so hot air can circulate around each piece.
  • Place the pan in an oven preheated to 425°F. Roast for 45 to 60 minutes, flipping every 15–20 minutes to ensure even browning.
  • Remove when the wings are deeply browned and crispy. Toss immediately in your choice of sauce or serve them as a dry rub.
